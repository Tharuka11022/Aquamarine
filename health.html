Detailed Coverage

1

Initial Health Window

The owner must have the pet examined by a licensed veterinarian within 72 hours of arrival. This validates the warranty and ensures a professional baseline for your pet's health.

2

Viral Illness Guarantee

If the pet is diagnosed with a life-threatening viral disease (such as Parvovirus for dogs or PBFD for birds) within the first 14 days, Aquamarine will provide a full replacement or cover authorized medical costs.

3

Genetic Integrity

Should a life-threatening hereditary defect be discovered within 12 months, we provide a replacement of equal value. Documentation from two independent veterinarians is required for genetic claims.

The Owner's Pledge

The health of a companion is a partnership. This warranty is valid only if the pet is provided with a safe environment, age-appropriate nutrition, and recommended vaccinations. It does not cover accidental injury, neglect, or common minor ailments such as stress-induced parasites which are easily treatable upon arrival.
