Delivery Coverage

Aquamarine provides professional pet logistics across the entire island. Our delivery zones are divided to ensure maximum speed:

  • Colombo & Suburbs: Same-day delivery available for orders placed before 10:00 AM.
  • Island-wide: Scheduled VIP transport within 24-48 hours depending on species requirements.

Professional Packaging

We use industry-leading bio-secure packaging tailored to each specific companion:

  • Aquatics: Double-layered leak-proof bags with medical-grade oxygen and thermal insulation.
  • Birds: Stress-reducing darkened transport carriers with non-slip flooring and hydration access.
  • Exotics: Secure, escape-proof, and ventilated specialized transit containers.

Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated based on distance and the complexity of the life-support systems required during transit.

  • Rates start from Rs. 500 for local deliveries.
  • Island-wide VIP specialized transport rates are provided during the final consultation.

The Safe Arrival Protocol

For your 100% Live Arrival Guarantee to be valid, we require a continuous, unedited unboxing video starting from the sealed package until the pet is visible. This helps us ensure the highest standards of safety for future journeys.
